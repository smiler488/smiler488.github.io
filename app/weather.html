Available NASA POWER Parameters

TOA_SW_DWN

Top-of-atmosphere shortwave downward irradiance

Unit: MJ/m²/day

ALLSKY_SFC_SW_DWN

All-sky surface shortwave downward irradiance

Unit: MJ/m²/day

T2M

Temperature at 2 meters

Unit: °C

T2M_MIN

Minimum temperature at 2 meters

Unit: °C

T2M_MAX

Maximum temperature at 2 meters

Unit: °C

T2MDEW

Dew point temperature at 2 meters

Unit: °C

WS2M

Wind speed at 2 meters

Unit: m/s

PRECTOTCORR

Precipitation corrected

Unit: mm/day
