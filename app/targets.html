Printing Instructions

  • Use high-quality white paper (minimum 80gsm recommended)
  • Print at 100% scale / Actual size - disable "Fit to page"
  • Use a laser printer for best precision and contrast
  • Verify printed dimensions with a ruler before use
  • For best results, mount on rigid backing (foam board, etc.)
