## About This Page

This resources page curates materials for the following research directions:

- Digital crop photosynthetic phenotyping
- Canopy light distribution and photosynthesis process modeling
- Multi-view 3D reconstruction and point-cloud phenotyping
- UAV remote sensing and multi-source data fusion
- AI-driven intelligent crop phenomics analysis
